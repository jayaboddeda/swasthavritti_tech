Documents

Standard Student Visa Documents Checklist

Core documents required across all destinations. Country-specific additions apply.

  • Valid passport (6+ months beyond course end date)
  • University offer/acceptance letter from a recognised institution
  • Proof of funds (bank statements, FD receipts, loan sanction letter)
  • English language test score - IELTS / PTE / TOEFL / OET
  • Academic transcripts (10th, 12th, Degree) - attested
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) - 750-1000 words
  • Letters of Recommendation (2-3 letters)
  • Passport-size photographs (as per embassy specifications)
  • Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) from Telangana Police
  • Medical examination certificate (where required)
  • TB test certificate (UK - from approved clinic in Hyderabad)
  • Country-specific docs: CAS (UK) - GIC (Canada) - CoE (Australia) - I-20 (USA)

Tip: We review your entire document package before submission. Our pre-check eliminates the most common cause of visa refusals - incomplete or inconsistent documentation.
