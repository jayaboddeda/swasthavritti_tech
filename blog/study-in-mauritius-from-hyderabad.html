Introduction

Mauritius is one of the most underrated study destinations for students from Hyderabad. A fully English medium education system, internationally recognised degrees, lower tuition and living costs, and a culturally familiar environment make it a highly practical option.

At Swasthavritti, we help Hyderabad students explore and apply to Mauritius universities as part of a smart and cost effective study abroad plan. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose Mauritius in 2026?

Fully English Medium Education

Mauritius follows a British influenced education system where English is the primary medium of instruction. Students face no language barrier in academics or daily life.

Lower Cost Compared to Western Countries

Tuition and living expenses are significantly lower than the USA, UK, Australia, or Canada, making it one of the most budget friendly international study options.

Internationally Recognised Degrees

Degrees from accredited Mauritius universities are recognised by the Association of Indian Universities. Many institutions also offer UK or Australian affiliated degrees.

Culturally Familiar Environment

A large Indian origin population means familiar food, festivals, and languages like Hindi and Tamil are widely present, making the transition easier.

Safe and Stable Country

Mauritius is known for its safety, political stability, and welcoming environment, making it ideal for first time international students.

Mauritius Student Visa Everything You Need to Know 2026

What is the Mauritius Student Visa

Students studying for more than 180 days require a student visa issued after arrival. Indian students typically enter visa free and complete the process in Mauritius.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport
  • Admission letter
  • Tuition fee payment proof
  • Financial proof
  • Accommodation proof
  • Medical certificate and insurance
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • Photographs
Entry Process

Indian students can enter Mauritius visa free for 60 days. After arrival, they apply for a Student Occupation Permit with the help of their university.

English Requirements for Mauritius Universities 2026

Most universities do not require IELTS for Indian students.

Some institutions may ask for IELTS 5.5 to 6.0 depending on the program.

Cost of Studying in Mauritius in 2026

  • Tuition fees: Rs 3 to Rs 8 lakhs per year
  • Living expenses: Rs 4 to Rs 6 lakhs per year
  • Total cost: Rs 7 to Rs 14 lakhs per year

This makes Mauritius one of the most affordable study abroad destinations.

Step by Step Process to Study in Mauritius from Hyderabad

  • Profile evaluation and university selection
  • Apply and receive offer letter within 2 to 4 weeks
  • Pay tuition deposit
  • Arrange accommodation
  • Obtain Police Clearance Certificate
  • Complete medicals and insurance
  • Book flights
  • Travel to Mauritius visa free
  • Apply for student permit after arrival
  • Attend pre departure briefing

Post Study Options After Studying in Mauritius

Graduates can apply for an Occupation Permit if they secure a job in Mauritius.

Many students also use their degree to pursue opportunities in India, the UK, Australia, or the Gulf.
