Introduction

New Zealand is quickly emerging as one of the most attractive study destinations for students from Hyderabad who want a world class education in a safe, peaceful, and affordable environment. With internationally recognised universities, strong post study work rights, and a welcoming immigration system, New Zealand offers excellent value compared to Australia, the UK, and the USA.

At Swasthavritti, we help Hyderabad students navigate the New Zealand student visa process from start to finish. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose New Zealand in 2026?

Safe and Peaceful Country

New Zealand consistently ranks among the safest countries in the world and offers one of the highest qualities of life. Low crime rates, a clean environment, and a welcoming atmosphere make it a preferred choice for students and parents.

Internationally Recognised Universities

New Zealand has eight government funded universities, all internationally accredited and recognised by the Association of Indian Universities.

  • University of Auckland ranks in the global top 100
  • Strong global recognition across Asia, Australia, and beyond
Generous Post Study Work Rights

After completing a degree of 2 years or more, students can apply for an open post study work visa of up to 3 years. This allows you to work for any employer anywhere in New Zealand.

More Affordable Than Australia and UK

Tuition fees and living costs are lower than Australia and significantly lower than the UK. This makes New Zealand a practical option for students seeking quality education without high financial pressure.

Work While You Study

Students can work up to 20 hours per week during semesters and full time during scheduled breaks, helping cover living expenses and gain work experience.

New Zealand Student Visa - Everything You Need to Know 2026

What is the New Zealand Student Visa

The student visa allows international students to study full time in New Zealand for programs longer than 3 months. Applications are submitted online through Immigration New Zealand.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport
  • Offer of place from a New Zealand institution
  • Proof of funds NZD 15,000 per year plus tuition
  • IELTS or PTE score
  • Medical and chest X ray certificates
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • Health and travel insurance
  • Passport size photographs
Visa Process

Applications are usually online with no interview. Additional documents or medicals may be requested. Swasthavritti ensures a complete and accurate application.

  • Undergraduate programs: 5.5 to 6.0

  • Competitive programs: 6.5 to 7.0
