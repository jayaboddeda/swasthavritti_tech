Introduction

Australia has consistently been one of the top three study destinations for students from Hyderabad. With world ranked universities, strong post study work rights, a clear PR pathway, and one of the highest qualities of life globally, studying in Australia is both an academic and lifestyle advantage.

At Swasthavritti, we guide Hyderabad students through every step of the Australian student visa process. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose Australia in 2026?

World Class Universities

Australia is home to the prestigious Group of Eight universities, including:

  • University of Melbourne
  • Australian National University
  • University of Sydney
  • University of Queensland

These institutions consistently rank in the global top 100 and are highly regarded by employers worldwide.

Strong Post Study Work Rights

After completing your degree, the Temporary Graduate visa Subclass 485 allows you to stay and work for 2 to 4 years depending on your qualification and location. Students in regional areas receive additional years.

Clear PR Pathway

Australia has a points based skilled migration system that offers a clear route to Permanent Residency. Fields like engineering, IT, nursing, accounting, and construction management are on the skilled occupation list, giving students strong PR opportunities.

Work While You Study

International students can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during semesters and unlimited hours during official breaks. This helps cover living costs and adds valuable work experience.

Large and Welcoming Indian Community

Australia has a large Indian community, especially in Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, and Perth. Telugu associations, Indian food, and cultural events make the transition easier for students from Hyderabad.

Australia Student Visa Subclass 500 Explained 2026

What is the Subclass 500 Student Visa

The Subclass 500 visa allows international students to study full time in Australia at a registered institution. It is the primary visa used by Indian students.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport
  • Confirmation of Enrolment
  • IELTS or PTE score
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant statement
  • Overseas Student Health Cover
  • Proof of financial capacity
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Passport size photographs
What is the GTE Statement

The Genuine Temporary Entrant statement explains why you want to study in Australia, your chosen course, and your intent to return to India. A strong GTE is critical for visa approval. Swasthavritti helps you prepare a compelling one.

IELTS Requirements for Australian Universities 2026

  • Undergraduate programs: 6.0 to 6.5 overall
  • Postgraduate programs: 6.5 overall
  • Competitive programs: 7.0 or above

PTE is also widely accepted.

Swasthavritti offers IELTS and PTE coaching in Hyderabad with mock tests and personalised feedback.

Cost of Studying in Australia in 2026

Tuition fees: Rs 15 to Rs 35 lakhs per year.

  • Sydney and Melbourne: Rs 12 to Rs 16 lakhs per year
  • Adelaide, Perth, Brisbane, Canberra: Rs 9 to Rs 13 lakhs per year
  • Overseas Student Health Cover is mandatory and costs approximately Rs 30,000 to Rs 60,000 per year

Scholarships for Indian Students in Australia

  • Australia Awards Scholarships fully funded
  • Destination Australia Scholarship up to AUD 15,000
  • Research Training Programme for research students
  • University specific merit scholarships

Australia Intake Dates for 2026

  • February 2026 intake: Main intake, widest programs. Deadlines September to November 2025
  • July 2026 intake: Secondary intake. Deadlines February to April 2026

Recommended intake: February.

Step by Step Process to Study in Australia from Hyderabad

  • Profile evaluation and university shortlisting 10 to 12 months before intake
  • Prepare for IELTS or PTE
  • Apply to universities
  • Receive offer letter
  • Accept offer and get Confirmation of Enrolment
  • Arrange Overseas Student Health Cover
  • Apply for Subclass 500 visa online
  • Submit biometrics and health checks if required
  • Await visa decision typically 4 to 6 weeks
  • Attend Swasthavritti pre departure briefing

Post Study Work Options in Australia

  • 2 years for major city graduates
  • Additional 1 to 2 years for regional graduates
  • 4 years for PhD graduates

After graduation, apply for the Temporary Graduate visa Subclass 485. You can build points for PR through the General Skilled Migration system via SkillSelect.
