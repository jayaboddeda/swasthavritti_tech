Introduction

The United Kingdom has always been one of the most sought-after study destinations for students from Hyderabad. With globally ranked universities, shorter degree durations, and a generous post-study work policy, studying in the UK is both prestigious and practical.

At Swasthavritti, we guide Hyderabad students through the entire UK student visa process, from university shortlisting to visa stamping. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose the UK in 2026?

World Class Universities

The UK is home to some of the most recognised universities in the world including Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College London, UCL, and London School of Economics.

A UK degree is respected by employers globally and opens doors across industries in India and abroad.

Shorter Degree Duration

One of the biggest advantages of studying in the UK is the duration. A Masters degree takes just 1 year compared to 2 years in the USA or Australia. This results in lower total tuition fees and living costs, and you enter the job market a full year earlier.

Graduate Route Visa - 2 Years Post Study Work

After completing your degree, the UK Graduate Route visa allows you to stay and work in the UK for 2 years, or 3 years if you completed a PhD. You do not need a job offer to apply.

Pathway to Long Term UK Stay

After the Graduate Route, you can switch to a Skilled Worker visa if you secure a qualifying job offer. Indefinite Leave to Remain becomes accessible after 5 years, making the UK a realistic long-term option.

Strong Indian Community

The UK has one of the largest Indian diaspora communities. Telugu associations, Indian restaurants, cultural events, and a familiar social environment exist in most major UK cities including London, Birmingham, Manchester, and Coventry.

UK Student Visa - Student Route Explained (2026)

What is the UK Student Route Visa?

The Student Route visa is for international students studying in the UK for more than 6 months. You must have a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from a licensed UK university.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • CAS number from your UK university
  • IELTS Academic score meeting university requirements
  • Proof of financial funds: GBP 1,334 per month for London, GBP 1,023 per month outside London (up to 9 months)
  • Academic transcripts, degree certificates, marksheets
  • TB test certificate (mandatory for Indian applicants)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Proof of parental consent if under 18
UK Student Visa Process

Most applicants submit online, provide biometrics at VFS Global Hyderabad, and wait for a decision. Some may be called for a credibility interview. Swasthavritti prepares students for both scenarios.

IELTS Requirements for UK Universities (2026)

  • Undergraduate programs: 6.0 to 6.5 overall
  • Postgraduate programs: 6.5 to 7.0 overall
  • Competitive programs (Medicine, Law, top MBA): 7.0 to 7.5 overall
  • Individual band score should not fall below 5.5 or 6.0 depending on the university

Cost of Studying in the UK in 2026

Tuition: Rs 15 to Rs 40 lakhs per year.

  • Living costs in London: Rs 12 to Rs 18 lakhs per year
  • Living costs outside London: Rs 8 to Rs 12 lakhs per year

Masters programs are 1 year, making the total cost lower than a 2-year US program. Cities like Coventry, Leeds, Sheffield, and Birmingham balance quality and affordability.

Scholarships for Indian Students in the UK

  • Chevening Scholarship - Fully funded, covers tuition, living costs, and flights
  • Commonwealth Scholarship - Fully funded for Indian students
  • GREAT Scholarships - Available at multiple UK universities
  • Universities also provide merit-based scholarships during admission

UK Intake Dates for 2026

September 2026: Main intake, widest program selection, maximum scholarships. Deadlines: January to June 2026.

January 2027: Secondary intake, limited programs. Deadlines: August to October 2026.

Recommended: September intake.

Step by Step Process to Study in UK from Hyderabad

  • Profile evaluation and university shortlisting 10 to 12 months before intake
  • Prepare and appear for IELTS Academic (2-3 months)
  • Apply to UK universities directly or via UCAS for undergraduate programs
  • Receive offer letter and CAS number
  • Apply for UK Student Route visa online through UKVI portal
  • Book biometrics appointment at VFS Global Hyderabad
  • Complete TB test at approved centre in Hyderabad
  • Submit all documents and await visa decision (usually 3 weeks)
  • Receive visa, book flights, arrange accommodation
  • Attend Swasthavritti pre-departure briefing covering banking, NHS registration, transport, and settling in UK life

Post-Study Work Options in the UK

  • Graduate Route visa: Stay and work in the UK for 2 years without a job offer (3 years for PhD)
  • Can switch to Skilled Worker visa if a qualifying job offer is received
  • Sets path to Indefinite Leave to Remain after 5 years
