Introduction

Canada has become one of the most sought after study destinations for students from Hyderabad in recent years and for good reason. Affordable tuition compared to the USA and UK, a clear pathway to Permanent Residency, and one of the most welcoming immigration systems make Canada a top choice.

At Swasthavritti, we have helped hundreds of Hyderabad students successfully obtain their Canada Study Permits. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose Canada in 2026?

Clear Pathway to Permanent Residency

Canada is one of the few countries where studying directly leads to a realistic PR pathway. After graduation, the Post Graduate Work Permit (PGWP) allows you to work for up to 3 years. This work experience qualifies you for the Canadian Experience Class under Express Entry, one of the fastest PR routes in the world with processing times as short as 6 months.

Affordable Tuition Compared to USA and UK

Canadian university tuition fees are significantly lower than equivalent programs in the USA or UK. Combined with the lower cost of living in cities outside Toronto and Vancouver, Canada offers excellent value for Hyderabad students seeking a high quality international education.

World Class Universities

Canada is home to globally recognised institutions including:

  • University of Toronto
  • McGill University
  • University of British Columbia
  • University of Waterloo
  • McMaster University

These universities rank consistently in the top 100 globally and are highly regarded by employers in India and internationally.

Safe and Welcoming Country

Canada ranks among the safest and most welcoming countries for international students. The country has a large and well established Indian community, particularly in cities like Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, and Ottawa, making the transition from Hyderabad smoother.

Work While You Study

International students can work up to 20 hours per week during semesters and full time during scheduled breaks without needing a separate work permit. This helps with living expenses and adds valuable Canadian work experience.

Canada Study Permit - Everything You Need to Know (2026)

What is a Canada Study Permit?

A Study Permit allows international students to study at a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) in Canada. It is not a visa. Most Indian students also need a Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) or Electronic Travel Authorisation (eTA) depending on their travel history.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport
  • Acceptance letter from a DLI
  • Proof of financial funds (tuition plus CAD 10,000 for living expenses)
  • IELTS score of 6.0 overall with no band below 6.0
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Academic transcripts and degree certificates
  • Proof of ties to India
  • Biometrics at VFS Global Hyderabad
  • Medical examination if required
SDS vs Regular Study Permit Stream
  • Student Direct Stream (SDS): Faster processing approximately 20 working days. Requires IELTS 6.0 in each band, CAD 10,000 GIC deposit, first year tuition paid, no visa refusals
  • Regular Stream: 8 to 12 weeks processing. More flexible financial documentation

IELTS Requirements for Canada (2026)

  • SDS: Minimum 6.0 in all bands
  • Most universities - Undergraduate: 6.0 to 6.5 overall
  • Most universities - Postgraduate: 6.5 to 7.0 overall
  • Top universities such as University of Toronto and UBC may require 6.5 to 7.0 overall

Cost of Studying in Canada in 2026

University tuition: Rs 10 to Rs 25 lakhs per year. College tuition: Rs 7 to Rs 15 lakhs per year.

  • Living expenses in expensive cities (Toronto, Vancouver): Rs 10 to Rs 14 lakhs per year
  • Living expenses in affordable cities (Kitchener, Hamilton, Calgary, London Ontario): Rs 6 to Rs 9 lakhs per year

Note: SDS GIC CAD 10,000 is a refundable deposit for initial living expenses, not a fee.

  • UBC International Leader of Tomorrow Award - Need based funding
  • Many colleges and universities offer entrance scholarships based on academic records

Canada Intake Dates for 2026

  • September 2026 (Fall): Main intake, wide program options. Deadlines: November 2025 to March 2026
  • January 2027 (Winter): Popular for colleges. Deadlines: August to October 2026
  • May 2027 (Summer): Limited programs at select colleges

Recommended: September intake for most students.

Step by Step Process to Study in Canada from Hyderabad

  • Profile evaluation and university or college shortlisting at least 10 to 12 months before intake
  • Prepare and appear for IELTS (allow 2 to 3 months)
  • Apply to DLI and receive acceptance letter
  • Open GIC account if applying under SDS, deposit CAD 10,000
  • Pay first year tuition if applying under SDS
  • Apply for Study Permit online through IRCC
  • Submit biometrics at VFS Global Hyderabad
  • Await Study Permit decision: 20 days for SDS, 8 to 12 weeks for Regular
  • Receive Study Permit approval, book flights, arrange accommodation
  • Attend pre departure briefing by Swasthavritti covering airport arrival, banking, SIN number, accommodation, and settling in Canada

PR Pathway After Studying in Canada

  • Post Graduate Work Permit (PGWP): Work up to 3 years after graduation
  • Canadian Experience Class: Eligible after 1 year of skilled Canadian work experience
  • PR process can take approximately 6 months
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP): Additional pathway with lower score requirements
