Introduction

Europe is fast becoming one of the most exciting study destinations for students from Hyderabad. With world ranked universities, low or zero tuition fees in countries like Germany, a growing number of English medium programmes, and access to 27 Schengen countries, Europe offers a unique academic and cultural experience.

At Swasthavritti, we help Hyderabad students navigate university applications and student visas for Germany, France, Ireland, and other European destinations. This guide covers everything you need to know for the 2026 intake.

Why Should Hyderabad Students Choose Europe in 2026?

Low or Zero Tuition Fees

Germany's public universities charge little to no tuition fees. Most institutions require only a semester fee of EUR 150 to EUR 350, which includes student services and transport. France also offers significantly lower tuition compared to the UK, USA, or Australia.

Schengen Zone Access

Studying in Europe allows you to travel across 27 countries without additional visas. This gives students exposure to multiple cultures, industries, and career opportunities.

English Medium Programmes

Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, and Ireland offer a wide range of English taught Masters programmes. Students can study fully in English while benefiting from European education systems.

Strong Job Market After Graduation

Germany has high demand for engineers, IT professionals, and healthcare workers. Ireland's tech sector is home to major global companies, creating strong job opportunities.

Pathway to Long Term Residency

Germany offers a Job Seeker Visa leading to work permits and residency. Ireland provides pathways through the Critical Skills Employment Permit. These options make Europe attractive for long term career plans.

Germany Student Visa National Visa Type D 2026

What is the Germany Student Visa

For studies longer than 90 days, Indian students must apply for a National Visa Type D. This is required before traveling to Germany.

Documents Required
  • Valid passport
  • University admission letter
  • APS certificate
  • Blocked account with EUR 11,208
  • IELTS or German language proof
  • Academic documents
  • Health insurance
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Visa application form and photographs
What is the APS Certificate

APS is a mandatory academic verification for Indian students applying to Germany. It validates your academic records and includes a short interview. Processing takes 6 to 8 weeks.

Ireland Student Visa Study in Ireland 2026

Students studying in Ireland for more than 90 days require a study visa.

  • Admission letter
  • Proof of funds
  • IELTS 6.0 to 6.5
  • Travel insurance

Processing time is typically 4 to 8 weeks.

Graduates can stay 12 to 24 months under the Third Level Graduate Programme.
