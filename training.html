Computer-Based

Results in 48 hours. Accepted for Australia PR, Canada visa, UK universities. AI-scored - no human bias.

Gold standard for MBA admissions. Required by top business schools in Canada, Australia, UK and USA.

Required for undergraduate admissions to USA colleges. Digital SAT format from 2024 - shorter and adaptive.

OET

Healthcare Professionals

Mandatory for nurses going to Australia (AHPRA), UK (NMC), Canada, NZ. Uses real healthcare scenarios.

  • OET letter writing templates
CAT

Gateway to IIMs and top Indian B-schools. Computer-based adaptive test covering VARC, DILR and Quantitative Ability.

  • Target: 95th+ percentile
  • Sectional mock tests
  • Time management strategies
